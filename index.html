MayDay is a Taiwanese band, consisting of five members: lead singer A-shin, head and guitarist Monster,
bassist Martha, guitarist Stone and drummer Guan You.With the exception of guanyu, all four graduated
from the affiliated high school of Taipei national normal university.Formerly known as "So Band", the
orchestra was renamed as "So Band" on March 29, 1997.
